About Me and My Projects

Some of the oldest memories I have are of tinkering with LEDs, cables and any PCB I could get my hands on. With no knowledge of what those components did, I sought to dismantle anything and everything tech I could get my hands on

Only years later I started building simple circuits on breadboards, playing with robotics and dabbling in code logic in software like Scratch

Now 21, I'm inspired by people such as Ben Eater, Harry Porter, Jon Stanly, DiPDoT and Clickety Clack to name a few, I started experimenting with circuit design myself.

I'm here to show that you don't need an Engineering Degree to build interesting things, trying to document my journey along the way.

~Just start doing something~
